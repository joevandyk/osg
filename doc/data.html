    • http://www.openscenegraph.org/download/data/OpenSceneGraphData-0.9.1.tar.gz

  • Terrain models in OpenFlight .flt and TerraPage format go to the download page at
    • http://www.terrex.com

  • Terrain models in OpenFlight .flt format at Grime:
    • http://www.grime.net

  • Various 3d models, many in .3ds format at 3D Cafe.
    • http://www.3dcafe.com

  • Various 3d models, many in .3ds format at 3D Rocket Fuel.
    • http://www.3drocketfuel.com

  • Vehicle models in OpenFlight .flt format at Full Sail (spinnercar & veritech):
    • http://www.fullsail.com/loader/models.html
